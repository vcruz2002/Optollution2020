As you can see in the graph above, industries are the number 1 source of air pollutants. This greatly impacts low-income communities of color because these companies try to help these communities financially, yet affecting thier environment. One city that is really picked on is Richmond, California. Richmond is a very low-income community made up of about 40% Latino and 5% White. In 2012, a crude-oil pipeline sparked a fire at the Cheveron refinery that sent more than 15,000 people, including both employees and those in the surrounding area, to the hospital for treatment.
